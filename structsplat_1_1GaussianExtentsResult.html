std::unique_ptr<DataTable> splat::GaussianExtentsResult::extents {nullptr}

DataTable containing extent_x, extent_y, extent_z columns. To compute AABB for Gaussian i: minX = x[i] - extent_x[i], maxX = x[i] + extent_x[i] minY = y[i] - extent_y[i], maxY = y[i] + extent_y[i] minZ = z[i] - extent_z[i], maxZ = z[i] + extent_z[i]
